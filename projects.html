Projects

My freshman year of high school, I took part in the class project called the "I search." This project is the infamous project that everyone hears about for several years leading up. The big English project of Freshman year. Looking back it wasn't all that bad, and it most definitely seemed much more scary than it actually was. I talked about the Spanish Influenza and the recent Covid-19. I looked at the two and compared them. From vaccines to life style. It was very interesting. The project entailed lots of reading, intereviews, and many videos. I learned the creation of certain types of vaccines, and even a little about how vaccines can be use in cancer patients. I learned a lot and if anything, that is what I took away from it all.
